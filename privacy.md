# Privacy policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-30._

## Who we are

Coffee Quitter is an Android app published by Lareon. If you have any questions about this policy or about how we handle your data, contact us at <tony1987@gmail.com>.

## What we collect

Coffee Quitter stores the following information **on your device only**:

- Caffeine log entries — timestamp, drink type, milligram amount, volume in millilitres, optional size label, optional notes.
- App settings — daily limit, volume unit, currency, half-life override, sleep target time, drink price overrides.
- Auto-taper plan state, if you have created one.

We do not collect any of this on a server. The app does not transmit your caffeine log to Lareon, our employees, or any third party.

## What leaves your device

- **Subscription purchases.** When you buy a subscription, Google Play handles the entire payment transaction. We never see your payment details.
- **Subscription validation.** Once we integrate the RevenueCat SDK to manage subscription entitlements (planned for a future release), an anonymised App User ID and the transaction events Google Play forwards will be sent to RevenueCat solely so the app knows whether your subscription is active. RevenueCat does not receive your caffeine log or any personal information beyond the App User ID Google Play provides. See <https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy> for RevenueCat's own privacy policy.

## Tracking and analytics

Coffee Quitter does not use any analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry SDKs in this version of the app. If we add any in future releases, they will be opt-in only and clearly disclosed before activation.

## Children

Coffee Quitter is intended for users aged 13 and older, in line with the Google Play Store's minimum-age policy. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

## Your rights under UK GDPR

Because all of your in-app data is stored on your device, you can exercise your right to erasure simply by uninstalling the app — Lareon has no server-side data about you to delete. For other rights (access, portability, rectification, objection, complaint), please contact <tony1987@gmail.com> and we will respond within one month, in line with the UK GDPR.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office: <https://ico.org.uk/concerns/>.

## Updates to this policy

We will update this page when we make material changes — for example, when we integrate the RevenueCat SDK noted above. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Once the in-app notification capability supports it, substantive changes will trigger an in-app notice.
